Why Volunteer?

The frequency and intensity of disasters is increasing and often the individuals affected are disproportionately those who live in rural areas, have preexisting medical conditions, and are low income. Communities like those in Asheville, NC or Camp Mystic, Texas who suffered from the Kerr County floods are still in the eye of the storm and are in need of your help. By mobilizing you, our first responders and veterans, we can strive to give back.
